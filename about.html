    Madrasatul Al-Athariyah being a school based on impacting sound teachings and manners of both the Qur'an and Sunnah in students was established and situated in EDE, Osun State Nigeria in the year 2020 by Al-Ustadh Abu Ja'far Nurudeen Jum'ah who is a well seasoned student of knowledge in the sciences of Hadith and also a memorizer of the Qur'an. He has also studied and still studying under the tutelage of prominent scholars such as As-shaykh Ibrahim Abdur-Rauf Abu Naasir, Abu Sakeenah, Abu Hanuun among others.

    Heading into it's fourth year of establishment, the Madrasah has up to 4 sets of admissions with over 90 students in total. Through the years 4 years of establishment we have successfully produced over 10 students who are now memorizers of the Qur'an and other knowledge based texts. At Madrasatul Al-Athariyah, provisions are made both day and boarding students with separate standard hostel facilities for both males and females.

    Our main goal is to produce students who would (by the Grace of Allah) raise the flag of Islam high and proud by teaching it in it's prestine form and aslo excel in the mundane modern sciences, in the bid to achieve this, we have inculcated into the Madrasah's programs some basic and fundamental Islamic texts in various field such as Aqeedah (Creed), Fiqh, Seerah (Islamic History), Hadith among others for our studnets to memorize. Such texts include Al-Aqeedatul waasitiyah, Arba'uun An-Nawawi, Al-Urjuzah, Al-usul Ath-thalaatha, Nawaaqidul-Islam to name a few.

    The western aspect of knowledge is also not neglected, at Al-Athariyah, we take our students through core sciences subject such as Math, English, Biology, Chemistry, Physics and Further-Math based on the SSCE syllabus.
