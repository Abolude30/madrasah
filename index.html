Curriculum Overview

We offer excellent learning programs in Islamic and mundane sciences. Take a glance through.

QUR'AN

We have mannered and upright reciters of the Quran that nurture students to achieve the Memorisation of the Qur'an within a period of 3years in shaa Allaah.

Arabic Language

We employ Arabic as the language of instruction in Quran and all Islamic sciences. Our students are enjoined to always communicate with Arabic. We employ Durus Al Lughah Al Arabiyah, and Silsilah ta'leem Al Lugah Al Arabiyah for our arabic studies.

Islamic Sciences

Our students are taken through a full course of Islamic learning and orientation. Tawheed, Aqeedah, Hadeeth, Fiqh, Tafseer, based on the methodology of the pious predecessors of Islam.

Mathematics

When it comes to mathematics, then you got no other option than us. Sequential treatment of mathematical operations provides our learners the solid foundation to traverse the world of Mathematics

ICT

Our learners are exposed to microsoft office tools until they attain proficiency. We thereafter walk them through the world of python programming.

English Language

Our learner experience a packed and organised sessions of English grammar, literature, comprehension and composition. Delivered by seasoned and experienced English tutors.
